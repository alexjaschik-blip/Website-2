About

Alex Jaschik is a filmmaker and videographer working at the intersection of documentary storytelling, visual art, and cultural heritage.

Based in Athens, Alex brings a cinematic eye to every project — from intimate personal portraits to expansive landscape studies. His work is shaped by the quality of Mediterranean light, the weight of ancient stone, and the restless energy of modern Greek life.

As founder of Doors of Athens, Alex has developed a distinct visual language that celebrates the overlooked and the enduring: the geometry of old thresholds, the patina of decades, the quiet poetry of everyday architecture.

Doors of Athens

A visual archive of Athens' historic doorways — cataloguing centuries of craft, colour, and character.

Founded in 2022, Doors of Athens is a photographic and editorial project that turns the city's forgotten facades into a cultural record. Each door is a threshold: between past and present, between private and public, between memory and ruin.

The project has appeared in print and online publications, and continues to grow as a living document of Athens' layered architectural identity.
